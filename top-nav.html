Navigation "task bar"

While Windows has a bottom task bar, which we try to recreate here with our nav styling, you can choose to have the navigation be at the bottom or at the top.

You can switch between the .nav-top and .nav-bottom classes. With just the .nav class your naviagtion will be at the top and not be fixed or sticky.
